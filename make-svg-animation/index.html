Tracing Options

Straight Line Ratio: the average amount of straight lines used in the tracing process. Increasing this will result in more corners in the traced image.

Curved Line Ratio: the average amount of curved lines used in the tracing process. Increasing this will result in more curves in the traced image.

Color Samples: The number of different color samples taken out of the original image. These will be used when coloring the image.

Number of Colors: The number of different colors used when recoloring the traced image.
